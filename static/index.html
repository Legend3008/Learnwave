How it works

From upload to insight in four steps

A retrieval-augmented generation pipeline, simplified.

1

Upload

Drop PDF or video. FastAPI validates and routes to the right processor.

2

Parse & chunk

PyMuPDF extracts text + tables. Split into semantic chunks of 512 tokens.

3

Embed & index

BGE-M3 generates 1,024-dim vectors. ChromaDB indexes for fast retrieval.

4

Ask & answer

Hybrid search finds passages. Llama 3.2 generates a cited answer.
