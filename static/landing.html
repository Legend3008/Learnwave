How it works

From upload to insight in four steps

A retrieval-augmented generation pipeline, simplified.

1

Upload

Drop your PDF or video file. FastAPI validates and routes it to the right processor.

2

Parse & chunk

PyMuPDF extracts text and tables. Content is split into semantically meaningful chunks.

3

Embed & index

BGE-M3 generates 1,024-dimensional vectors. ChromaDB stores them for fast cosine retrieval.

4

Ask & answer

Hybrid search finds relevant passages. Llama 3.2 generates a cited answer from your documents.
